SEO Techniques

  1. Target Specific Keywords

    2. Researching the right keywords for a webpage can be useful as they can lead the viewers to the webpage in little time. By thinking about what words or phrases that viewers will use when searching for the webpage, the webpage will be easy to find and clicked on more often. The web designer can know what words to use to lead viewers to their webpage by researching what words are frequently entered in the search bar and their rankings. But the words must be related to the content of the webpage to be searched easily (Moz, n.d.).

  2. Design Search Appearance

    3. Designing a good search appearance is important for a web designer if they want viewers to click on their webpage. In addition to looking for key words of what the viewer is trying to research, they will look at the appearance of the search results and choose the one that appeals to them the most. Some factors that may influence this decision include: favicons, breadcrumbs, title, description, and rich results. Enhancing these elements are the key to designing a good search appearance that will sway the viewers into clicking the designer's webpage (Moz, n.d.).

  3. Create Quality Content

    4. Creating high quality content on a webpage is necessary for introducing a high volume of viewers to the webpage. This involves having close to or exactly the information that viewers wish to see. Also, the information must be laid out in a way that is easy to understand and absorb. This can be done by having a well structured webpage with information that is clearly pointed out with headers. The designer can also use images to provide visual meanings of the information to the viewer. Ensuring the website is neat with updated information will make it quick for the search engine and viewer to navigate to the webpage (Moz, n.d.).

SMO Techniques

  1. Create Shareable Content

    2. By creating content that viewers will like and enjoy, it will become popular overtime. Having content that the viewers can express their interest towards will influence them to share it with their friends and families. This is how more social relationships become developed when they have content that they relate to and talk about. This will boost the social platform's popularity as the origin of the popular content being shared to many viewers (Bhargava, 2010).

  2. Share Content Easily

    3. Sharing content must be made easily in order to boost a social platform's reputation and market share over the social media industry. Viewers must have the option to be able to share content with the touch of a button rather than tagging or bookmarking it. Some workarounds to these limited options can be to have a share button that lets one select the people they want to relay it to or adding a link to the content to their profiles for people to click and view it. Sharing content easily will increase viewers' satisfaction and promote social platform's operations (Bhargava, 2010).

  3. Encourage Free Mashup

    4. Encouraging the mashup is important because it keeps the content alive and heard to many viewers. This would promote viewer engagement, creativity, and wide content distribution to different social platforms. If viewers are able to freely remix the content and turn it into their own, it will give them a sense of ownership and a personal connection to it. This would increase the chances of them sharing it to others and creating exposure to the original content. In addition, mashups introduce an inclusive environment where everyone's opinions are heard and this can provide feedback on the viewers' perspective towards the content (Bhargava, 2010).
